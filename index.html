Mohammad Fakrul Alam (@rapappu) is Senior System Engineer at NTT Ltd. Before joining NTT Ltd, Fakrul was Senior Network Engineer at Digital Armour. Prior to that he worked for APNIC as Senior Network Analyst where he was responsible for the development and delivery of technical training to the APNIC community and works closely with network operating members in the Asia Pacific region as well as maintaining APNIC Training Lab.


Fakrul worked for several organization which includes IXP, ISP, Financial Institutes. He has strong knowledge of, and operational experience in building and deploying scalable, reliable network infrastructure.


Founding member of bdCERT (Bangladesh Computer Emergency Response Team) and bdNOG (Bangladesh Network Operators Group)


Active speaker in different international conference which includes APRICOT, SANOG, PACNOG, IDNOG and other regional NOG's and security conferences.
